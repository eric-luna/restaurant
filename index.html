Fresh. Bold. Inspiring.

HodgePodge offers good food and good music at the same time. This restaurant prides itself on its menu being fresh, organic, with food being prepared in a simple manner in order to satisfy a variety of tastes.

About our food

We like simple, yet flavorful recipes and believe marinating is not to be taken lightly. We know medium-rare isn’t the only way to cook a steak, but believe it should be.Honest food and honest drinks set the foundation of our menu. While dishes have roots from all four corners of the country,our menu integrates a range of far-flung flavors from across the globe to keep palates guessing.

Fresh Ingredients

When fresh food is in season and ripe off the vine, there's nothing more delicious. People love to savor the genuine wholesomeness that comes from fresh ingredients. Fresh foods deliver a bright, lively experience that can be just as addictive as salt, sugar, or fat (but in a good way!).

World-Class Chefs

Our chefs aim to give you a rich experience. To explore some of the most beautiful, natural produce. To present an original cuisine where texture, purity of flavour and balance is paramount. To bring warmth and knowledge to our customers.

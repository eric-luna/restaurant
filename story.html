Our Story

Our founder, Eric Luna, had an idea to create a restaurant that was more than a place that served great food. Eric wanted to give locals a place where they'd always feel at home. A place that provided no-nonsense food at down-to-earth prices, and where they'd be treated right by people who had a passion to serve.
